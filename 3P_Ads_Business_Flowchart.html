Strategic Value Proposition

Why Advertisers Pay Razorpay

  • Behavioral Precision: Insurance buyers with specific income bands
  • Cross-Platform Reach: User behavior across multiple merchant sites
  • Intent Data: Payment completion = highest intent signal
  • Better Performance: RZP segments 2-26x better ROAS vs publisher segments

Why Publishers Participate

  • Incremental Revenue: Additional income from existing inventory
  • Better Targeting: Access to cross-merchant behavioral data
  • No Build Costs: Don't need own lookalike modeling

Why Razorpay Wins

  • Low Marginal Cost: Data is byproduct of payment business
  • High Margins: ~20-30% estimated
  • Network Effects: More merchants ‚Üí better segments ‚Üí more advertisers
  • Ecosystem Deepening: Stronger merchant retention
  • Privacy-Compliant: Aggregate cohorts, not individual data
